ABOUT US

Welcome to INDEVAInnovative Development Association, where innovation meets education, youth empowerment, and cross-cultural exchange through our dynamic Erasmus projects. As a leading non-profit organization, we are committed to reshaping the landscape of education and fostering the development of tomorrow's leaders.

At INDEVA, we believe in the transformative power of innovation to revolutionize educational practices. By harnessing cutting-edge technologies, creative methodologies, and forward-thinking approaches, we strive to create engaging and inclusive learning environments that inspire curiosity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning among youth.

WHO WE ARE?

Driven by a team of 7 dedicated professionals and volunteers, INDEVA is committed to making a tangible difference in the lives of youth worldwide. With a focus on innovation, education, and Erasmus projects, we are shaping the future of learning and empowering young people to realize their full potential. Join us on our journey as we continue to inspire, innovate, and ignite change in the field of education and youth development. Together, we can create a brighter, more inclusive world for generations to come.

OUR MISSION

Central to our mission is our dedication to youth work. We recognize the immense potential within young people and are passionate about providing them with the tools, resources, and opportunities they need to thrive. Through our diverse range of programs, workshops, and initiatives, we empower young individuals to discover their passions, cultivate their talents, and become active contributors to their communities.

PROJECTS

One of the cornerstones of our work is our involvement in Erasmus projects. Through international collaboration and exchange, we bridge cultures, break down barriers, and foster mutual understanding among young people from different backgrounds. Our Erasmus projects not only provide participants with invaluable intercultural experiences but also equip them with the skills and competencies needed to navigate an increasingly globalized world.
